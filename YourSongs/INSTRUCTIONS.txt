📁 YourSongs Folder

All audio files (.wav) generated by RhythmCode will be automatically saved in this folder. Here you will find:

🎵 Your musical compositions created with the compiler.

⏳ A history of your projects (each run generates a new file).

📝 How does it work?

Automatic generation:

When you compile your code with RhythmCode, the .wav files will be saved here with the name you specify (e.g., my_song.wav).
